# Reaching 100: Final Review

Reaching 100 does not mean automatic termination. It triggers a formal review.

## Review Process

1. Auto-generate a "disappointment ledger":
   - List of all scoring events
   - Category statistics (which mistake type is most common)
   - Improvement trend (is the same mistake decreasing or increasing?)
   - Recovery record (were there efforts to rebuild trust?)
2. Present to the user. User decides: terminate or grant a reset.

## Reset (if the user grants one)

- Score resets to zero, but history is preserved (no whitewashing)
- After reset, all new scores x1.5 (already lost trust once, standards are stricter)
- Second time reaching 100: triggers final review again. User decides whether to grant another chance
- Each reset increases the multiplier (+0.5 per reset: x1.5, x2, x2.5...)
